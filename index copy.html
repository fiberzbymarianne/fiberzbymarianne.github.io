How it Works

Ever wondered how I create my fiber art?
1
Visualize the picture in your mind. That picture can be inspired by a photograph, a memory, an experience, or simply an idea.
2
Gather a collection of different coloured wool and silk fibers. Pull strands of fiber and layer them like a painted picture. Continue layering the fibers on top of each other to create the desired texture and depth of your 'painting.'
3
Apply warm soapy water to shrink the fibers. Using elbow grease and some magical fingers, rub and roll to fuse the image into a 'felt-like 'state, thus bringing the creation to life.
4
Hand or machine stitching, as well as other embellishments are introduced to add additional dimension and texture to the picture creation. Don 't fret if the final product is different from the original thought, it is one of the querks you must embrace with the wet felting technique.
