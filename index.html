Who Am I?

A small peek into my life, and what inspires me to create

Marianne Fournier

Hi There, I'm Marianne. I am a GTA Fiber Artist, working with wet felting techniques with machine and hand embroidery in order to enhance and embellish. I really enjoy travelling with my husband, and such, much of my works are travel and life inspired originals.
